About Us

At Sema na Mimi (Talk to Me), we believe that every child deserves to be heard and understood. Our mission is to break down communication barriers for deaf children across Kenya by making sign language learning fun, accessible, and community-driven.

Founded in 2023 by a team of educators, technologists, and deaf advocates, our platform combines playful learning experiences with practical tools that make a real difference in children's daily lives.
